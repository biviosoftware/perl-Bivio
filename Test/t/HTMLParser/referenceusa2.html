"Company Name" Search offers three Levels:

Level 1 - Initial Search: Level 1 is an "exact match" word search. For example, when you search by the word "car", the results will be all businesses that have the word "car" in the company name.

Example: "Gari's Car Repair" and "Marge's Car Service".

Level 2 - Wildcard (*) Search: When Level 1 results are returned a "Try Harder - Wildcard (*)" button will appear in red. Clicking on this button will append a wildcard (*) on each word in the "Company Name" search field. Results are then blended with the results from the Level 1 search. Searching by "car" and then clicking "Try Harder - Wildcard (*)" will return results with the word "car" somewhere in the company title.

Example: "Liz's Daycare", "Jane's Carpet Cleaners" or "Terese's Carousel".

Level 2 will also return results with company names that have your search terms in any order.

Level 3 - "Sounds Like" Search: When results are returned after a Level 2 search a "Try Harder - Sounds Like" button appears. Clicking on this button expands your search to return results that sound like your search word(s). This Level 3 search returns a new and separate list of results from the first two Levels.

Example: When searching by "fizisions" no results will be found in Level 1 or 2. The results on a Level 3 search will include businesses with the word "physicians" in the company name."
