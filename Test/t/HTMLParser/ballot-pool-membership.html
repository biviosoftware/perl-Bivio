MyBallot Control Panel >> Manage Ballot Pool Memberships
 
Ballot pools which you currently belong to have a check in the Enrolled column. To update your ballot pools, select or clear the checkbox in the row next to the Sponsor, and then select the Update button at the bottom of the page.
 
As standards projects from within specific disciplines are nearing the Sponsor Ballot stage, individuals who belong to the relevant balloting pool will receive an "Invitation to Ballot". Those who respond positively to these Invitations (and who are IEEE Standards Association members or pay a per-ballot fee) will then form the Balloting Group for that project.
